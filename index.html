About Foundation

हाम्रो बारेमाAbout Us

Shalom Guru Foundation शिक्षा, स्वास्थ्य, वातावरणीय सरसफाइ, सामाजिक उत्थान, सीप विकास, गरिबी न्यूनीकरण र मानव अधिकारसम्बन्धी जनचेतना फैलाउने उद्देश्यले स्थापना गरिएको संस्था हो।
Shalom Guru Foundation is established to promote education, health, environmental cleanliness, social upliftment, skill development, poverty reduction, and human rights awareness.

हाम्रो लक्ष्यOur Mission

शिक्षा, स्वास्थ्य, सरसफाइ, वातावरण संरक्षण र सामाजिक समावेशीकरणका माध्यमबाट सम्मानजनक र अवसरयुक्त जीवन निर्माण गर्ने।
To help create dignified and opportunity-filled lives through education, health, cleanliness, environmental care, and social inclusion.

हाम्रो दृष्टिOur Vision

प्रत्येक व्यक्ति, परिवार र समुदायले सुरक्षित, स्वस्थ, सफा र समावेशी वातावरणमा जीवनयापन गर्न सक्ने नेपाल निर्माण गर्नु।
To build a Nepal where every person, family, and community can live in a safe, healthy, clean, and inclusive environment.

Objectives

11 Main Objectives११ मुख्य उद्देश्यहरू

1. Education Support

Scholarships, school materials, literacy, and educational access.

2. Health Services

Health camps, checkups, medicine support, and awareness programs.

3. Environmental Protection

Tree plantation, clean water, waste management, and conservation activities.

4. Cleanliness Campaigns

Community sanitation, public cleaning drives, and hygiene awareness.

5. Skill Development

Vocational training, entrepreneurship, and livelihood improvement.

6. Women Empowerment

Programs supporting women's education, safety, skills, and leadership.

7. Child Protection

Support for children, orphans, vulnerable groups, and child rights awareness.

8. Disaster Relief

Emergency response, rescue support, food, shelter, and relief distribution.

9. Social Inclusion

Support for marginalized, disabled, and disadvantaged communities.

10. Human Rights Awareness

Promoting equality, dignity, justice, and legal awareness.

11. Community Development

Supporting long-term local development and stronger communities.

Our Work

📘

शिक्षा सहयोगEducation Support

छात्रवृत्ति, शैक्षिक सामग्री, विद्यार्थी सहयोग र शिक्षाबाट वञ्चित समूहमा पहुँच।

स्वास्थ्य तथा कल्याणHealth & Wellness

Health camps, clinics, checkups, and support for financially vulnerable patients.
🌿

वातावरण तथा सरसफाइEnvironment & Cleanliness

वृक्षारोपण, सरसफाइ अभियान, पानी व्यवस्थापन र स्वस्थ वातावरण निर्माणका कार्यक्रम।
Tree plantation, cleanup campaigns, water management, and healthy environment programs.
