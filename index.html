About Foundation

A Foundation Built on Serviceसेवामा आधारित Foundation

Shalom Guru Foundation is a service-driven organization committed to education support, health access, environmental care, cleanliness, social upliftment, and community empowerment. The foundation is built to serve with compassion, credibility, and long-term impact.
Shalom Guru Foundation शिक्षा, स्वास्थ्य, वातावरणीय सरसफाइ, सामाजिक उत्थान, सीप विकास र सामुदायिक सशक्तिकरणप्रति समर्पित सेवा-आधारित संस्था हो।

Mission

To create dignified, opportunity-filled lives through service, compassion, education, health support, cleanliness, and environmental responsibility.
सेवा, करुणा, शिक्षा, स्वास्थ्य, सरसफाइ र वातावरणीय जिम्मेवारीमार्फत सम्मानजनक जीवन निर्माण गर्ने।

Vision

To build a cleaner, healthier, more just, and more inclusive Nepal where every community can grow with dignity.
प्रत्येक समुदायले सम्मानका साथ अघि बढ्न सक्ने सफा, स्वस्थ, न्यायपूर्ण र समावेशी नेपाल निर्माण गर्ने।

Objectives

11 Main Objectives११ मुख्य उद्देश्यहरू

1. Education Support

Scholarships, learning materials, literacy promotion, and education access for underserved groups.

2. Health Services

Health camps, checkups, medicine support, and health awareness activities.

3. Environmental Protection

Tree plantation, clean water, waste management, and conservation initiatives.

4. Cleanliness Campaigns

Community sanitation, public cleaning drives, and hygiene awareness programs.

5. Skill Development

Vocational training, practical skills, entrepreneurship, and livelihood improvement.

6. Women Empowerment

Programs supporting women’s safety, education, confidence, skills, and leadership.

7. Child Protection

Support for children, vulnerable groups, and child rights awareness.

8. Disaster Relief

Emergency response, food, shelter, rescue support, and relief distribution.

9. Social Inclusion

Support for marginalized, disabled, disadvantaged, and excluded communities.

10. Human Rights Awareness

Promoting dignity, equality, justice, legal awareness, and civic responsibility.

11. Community Development

Supporting long-term local development, resilience, and stronger communities.
