About the Foundation

A new beginning with a clear purpose.

Shalom Guru Foundation has been registered for social services in Nepal. The foundation exists to support human dignity, social welfare, empowerment, and community development through organized service and long-term vision.

Our Mission

To serve people with compassion, responsibility, and practical support while building a strong path for future humanitarian and community-focused work.

Our Vision

To become a respected social foundation in Nepal that creates meaningful, lasting, and positive impact in communities through service and unity.

Our Values

Integrity, dignity, compassion, faith, transparency, service, empowerment, and commitment to helping those in need.
